About Me

Hi! My name is Chyniece and I'm a student at Code Crew where I am currently learning all the things about software development. My hope is to become a well rounded fullstack engineer. I am passionate about The Arts and I aim to incorpate that with coding.

Honestly, I did not expect to be where I am now considering my background mainly consists of The Arts. Being born in Memphis, the city of the blues, it was natural that I leaned toward The Arts. I've danced for most of my life but once I knew I no longer was passionate about pursuing a career in it, I pursued other avenues. That quickly turned into me wanting to know more about coding as I was introduced to Excel, SQL and then Python.

My hobbies include hot yoga, taking dance classes, traveling, listening to music, and anything beauty related.

I'm not a fan of sports but I consider dance a sport becuause its a very physical activity.
